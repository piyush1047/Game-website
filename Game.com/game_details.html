PUBG MOBILE

PUBG Mobile is a global mobile video game developed by TapTap Games and published by Ubisoft. It was released on 11 September 2019 for Android and iOS, and on 24 October 2020 for Windows 10 and Windows Phone. PUBG Mobile is a survival game that combines elements of first-person shooter, action-adventure, and strategy, with a focus on teamwork and cooperation. It is the second installment in the PUBG series, following PUBG and PUBG Battlegrounds.
